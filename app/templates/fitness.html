Your Workout Journey

STEP 01 Set Your Profile

Enter your height, weight, age, and fitness goal. Our system instantly computes your BMI, BMR, and recommended daily caloric targets to build a personalized baseline.

STEP 02 Receive Your Plan

Your trainer-admin assigns a fully customized weekly workout schedule with detailed exercises, duration, calorie targets, and difficulty levels tailored to your goals.

STEP 03 Execute & Log

Complete your workouts and log each session. Track sets, reps, duration, and calories burned. Mark plans as complete, skip, or view full instructions with photo guides.

STEP 04 Analyze & Evolve

View detailed analytics on your performance trends, calorie burn history, and goal progress. Your plan evolves as you improve, keeping you challenged and growing.
